IUTF — Integrated Urban Traffic + Rainfall (40 cities)
=======================================================
This dataset is too large for automatic download (~10GB).

Download manually from Figshare:
    https://doi.org/10.6084/m9.figshare.30022807.v1

Click "Download all" on the Figshare page.

Save the extracted files to this folder:
    C:\Users\agnes\Downloads\Capstone\weather_env\data\05_traffic\iutf_rainfall_traffic

What you get:
  - Hourly traffic counts from 21,700+ sensors
  - ERA5 hourly rainfall aligned to each sensor
  - OSM road network topology
  - Covers 40 global cities (2015-2017)
  - Pre-integrated: no joining needed

Paper: https://www.nature.com/articles/s41597-025-06336-3
